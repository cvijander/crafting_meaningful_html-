How does extra virgin olive oil (EVOO) compare to regular olive oil?

EVOO is made from pure, cold-pressed olives and is certified as free of any defects in flavor or odor, when tested by a tained panel of exprerts.Regular olive oil is made from a blend of cold-pressed and processed oils.
